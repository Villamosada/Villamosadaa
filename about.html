About Boracay Island

Boracay Island is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the Philippines. It is located in the province of Aklan and is famous for its powdery white sand, crystal-clear waters, and breathtaking sunsets.

History

Before becoming a world-famous destination, Boracay was a quiet island inhabited by local communities. Over time, it gained international recognition for its beauty and became a top travel destination.

Why Visit Boracay?

  • Beautiful white sand beaches
  • Clear and calm waters
  • Exciting water activities
  • Vibrant nightlife

Fun Facts

  • Boracay is only about 7 kilometers long
  • White Beach is divided into Stations 1, 2, and 3
  • It was once named one of the best islands in the world
